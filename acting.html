About Tim

Tim Barge is a Los Angeles-based actor with a background in comedy and a knack for making branded content feel human. He brings a natural, grounded performance style to commercial work — the kind that reads as real rather than rehearsed.

Whether he's playing the straight man, the goofy neighbor, or the guy who really loves this product a little too much, Tim finds the funny without losing the truth of the moment.

He's worked with brands across consumer goods, tech, food & beverage, and lifestyle — and he's comfortable in everything from scripted spots to improv-heavy formats.
